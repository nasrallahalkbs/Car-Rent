Our Story

Founded in 2010, CarRental began with a small fleet of just 10 vehicles and a big vision: to transform the car rental experience by putting customer convenience and satisfaction at the forefront of everything we do.

2010

CarRental founded with our first location in downtown

2015

Expanded to 5 locations across the state with a fleet of 100+ vehicles

2018

Launched our mobile app for seamless booking and digital check-in

2020

Added electric vehicles to our fleet and implemented contactless services

Today

Operating in 15 cities with a diverse fleet of 500+ vehicles and cutting-edge technology
